             CHESS HUB - SOUND FILE SPECIFICATIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Required Files (Add all 5 to this Sounds folder):
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. move.mp3 (or move.wav)
   ├─ Purpose: Regular piece movement
   ├─ Duration: 0.2-0.5 seconds
   ├─ Style: Soft, quick click or slide
   ├─ Reference: Light tap on wooden board
   └─ Plays at: 60% volume

2. capture.mp3 (or capture.wav)
   ├─ Purpose: Piece captures another piece
   ├─ Duration: 0.2-0.5 seconds
   ├─ Style: Sharper, more pronounced than move
   ├─ Reference: Firm piece placement
   └─ Plays at: 80% volume

3. castle.mp3 (or castle.wav)
   ├─ Purpose: Castling move (king + rook)
   ├─ Duration: 0.3-0.6 seconds
   ├─ Style: Double click or distinctive sound
   ├─ Reference: Two pieces moving simultaneously
   └─ Plays at: 70% volume

4. success.mp3 (or success.wav)
   ├─ Purpose: Puzzle solved correctly
   ├─ Duration: 0.5-1.5 seconds
   ├─ Style: Positive chime, victory bell
   ├─ Reference: Level complete sound
   └─ Plays at: 90% volume

5. failure.mp3 (or failure.wav)
   ├─ Purpose: Wrong move made
   ├─ Duration: 0.3-0.8 seconds
   ├─ Style: Short buzz, gentle error tone
   ├─ Reference: Incorrect answer buzz (not harsh!)
   └─ Plays at: 70% volume

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Audio Format:
  • Format: MP3 (recommended) or WAV
  • Sample Rate: 44.1 kHz
  • Bit Rate: 128-192 kbps (MP3)
  • Channels: Mono or Stereo (Mono is fine)
  • Max File Size: < 200 KB each

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Installation:
  1. Drag all 5 files into this Sounds folder in Xcode
  2. Check "Copy items if needed"
  3. Select "Chess Hub" target
  4. Choose "Create folder references"
  5. Build and run!

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Sound Suggestions (by priority):

Essential (Must have good sounds):
  • move.mp3 - Most frequently played
  • capture.mp3 - Important feedback for tactics
  • castle.mp3 - Distinctive for special move

Nice to have (Can use simpler sounds):
  • success.mp3 - Plays only on puzzle completion
  • failure.mp3 - Plays on wrong moves

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Testing After Installation:

Play a puzzle and verify:
  ✓ Selecting piece = Subtle vibration
  ✓ Moving piece = "move.mp3" sound
  ✓ Capturing piece = "capture.mp3" sound
  ✓ Castling = "castle.mp3" sound (king moves 2 squares)
  ✓ Wrong move = "failure.mp3" + strong vibration + red flash
  ✓ Puzzle complete = "success.mp3" + celebration vibration

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Quick Tips:

• Keep sounds SHORT to avoid overlap
• Test on real device at different volumes
• Sounds should feel satisfying but not annoying
• Match the app's elegant aesthetic
• Chess.com and Lichess are great references
